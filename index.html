Essential Travel Tips

Best Time to Visit

Spring (April-May) and Fall (September-October) offer pleasant weather and fewer crowds.

Currency

The ancient Romans used denarii and sestertii, but today's visitors should bring Euros.

Language

While Latin was the original language, modern Italian and English are widely spoken today.
